Our Story

MedicoTech™ is the software division of That Psychologist Thato (Pty) Ltd, founded by clinical Psychologist Thato Masia. Born from the realities of modern clinical practice, MedicoTech exists to support the professionals behind the practice by streamlining complex and time-consuming report writing.

Our Mission

To empower healthcare professionals with structured software that saves time, supports accuracy, and upholds professional standards.

Our Vision

To become Africa's leading provider of intelligent, ethical, and expert-driven medical report writing tools.

Meet Our Director

Thato Masia

Director | Clinical Psychologist | Entrepreneur

Psychologist Thato Masia is rewriting the conversation around mental health—literally and figuratively. Known for her clear, relatable voice on national TV and digital platforms, she brings authority and empathy to every discussion, making complex mental health topics simple, practical, and human. At just 29, Thato has become a trusted voice in South Africa's mental health space, appearing on SABC and other major channels where her insightful analysis and authentic delivery continue to resonate with audiences.

Recognised as an Allan Gray Fellow, Thato brings entrepreneurial energy to psychology. She is the founder of MedicoTech™, the company that created Psyche-Writer™ and Psychometry-Writer™. Designed by clinicians for clinicians, these tools help professionals focus more on patients, not paperwork. She continues to engage top experts—including Prof. Solly Rataemane, Dr. Itumeleng Funani, and Dr. Jacob Poo—while sharing her insights on platforms that shape public opinion.

Today, Thato continues to engage top experts—including Prof. Solly Rataemane, a renowned psychiatrist and former Head of the Department of the HPCSA’s Medical and Dental Board, Head of Psychiatry at Sefako Makgatho Health Sciences University and Dr George Mukhari Academic Hospital; Dr. Itumeleng Funani, a seasoned consultant with over a decade of experience in health governance, strategy development, and partnerships across South Africa’s Department of Health and donor networks; and Dr. Jacob Poo, a healthcare leader formerly associated with MEDITECH South Africa and currently practicing at Dr George Mukhari Academic Hospital, known for his work in digital health systems and clinical care —while sharing her insights on platforms that shape public opinion.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide everything we do.

Clinical Integrity

We never automate clinical judgment. Our tools enhance, never replace, professional expertise.

Data Ethics

Patient confidentiality and data protection are fundamental to our design philosophy.

Collaboration

We work closely with clinicians to ensure our solutions address real-world challenges.

Innovation

We continuously evolve our tools to meet the changing needs of healthcare professionals.

Compliance

All our tools are designed to meet HPCSA, POPIA, and other regulatory requirements.
