How Psyche-Writer™ Works

1. Sectioned Reporting

Psyche-Writer™ breaks the report into clearly defined, logically sequenced sections that guide the expert through each step of the documentation process.

2. Expert-Led Input

The tool does not generate clinical interpretations. All diagnostic reasoning and interpretative content must be entered by the clinician.

3. Justification Prompts

When a clinician enters an interpretation, the tool offers idiosyncratic applications and collateral justifications that can be accepted, edited, or dismissed.

4. Redaction Tools

Built-in prompts and formatting tools assist clinicians in removing identifying patient information, such as names and facility locations.
