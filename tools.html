Psyche-Writer™

The industry standard for AI-assisted reporting in medico-legal and forensic neuropsychological assessments.

Reduce Report Writing to Less Than 1 Hour

Each report is customizable by the clinician. From editable sections to expandable commentary fields, professionals stay in control of tone, discursive style and interpretation. The tool does not generate clinical interpretations or draw conclusions; all diagnostic reasoning and interpretative content must be entered by the clinician.
